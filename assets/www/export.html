Trough our partner company ALLEXPRESS we can warehouse, distribute and ship your cargo worldwide.

At ALLEXPRESS our Customer Service Representatives speak English, Portuguese and Spanish and will prepare all necessary documents to expedite your shipments, make sure they are documented accurately and according to your diversified products, as well as provide you with the best air and ocean rates in the market.
